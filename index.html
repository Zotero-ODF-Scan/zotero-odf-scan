RTF/ODF Scan for Zotero

This plugin is a companion to Zotero (or Multilingual Zotero) that adds the ability to convert Scannable Cite markers in an ODF document (the native format of LibreOffice) to fully functional Zotero citations. Conversion works in both directions, and documents can contain a mixture of Scannable Cite markers and "live" Zotero citations. Conversion will transform all cites in the document to the selected form. When converting to live citations, adjacent markers will be merged into "multiple" Zotero citations.
